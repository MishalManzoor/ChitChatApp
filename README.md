
# Chit Chat

Chit Chat is a cool messaging app that keeps your chats safe and quick. It uses Firebase to make logging in easy and keeps your friend list and chats updated in real-time. Connect with friends instantly and personalize your profile easily. Your chats are private and secure, and you can use the app on different devices.
